## User Stories

John is a recently laid-off worker located in Seattle.
He wants to find emergency food banks and meals near him to feed his family during their time of transition.
He wants to find accessible routes to operational food banks and meals. 
He needs to be able to filter for specific food bank requirements that matches his family needs, like meals and serving children.
John's job does not have technical skills and values a simple user interface. 



Jane is a food bank manager. 
She needs to see existing food bank locations and public transportation routes. 
She wants to see where she should open a new food bank location that will serve an under-served area of Seattle that is accessible via public transportation. 
Jane's job does not involve technical skills and she values a simple user interface. 


Carl is a department head for SoundTransit. 
Carl wants to suggest a new public transportation line to serve new areas within Seattle and King County. 
Carl wants to suggest areas that would increase accessibility to emergency food programs. 
Carl needs to see operational food programs within Seattle. 
Carl has knowledge of existing transit routes. Carl's job does not involve technical skills and he values a simple user interface.
